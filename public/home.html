Our mission

With 1.3 billion tons of food wasted each year it only makes sense to put some of that in the hands of the less fortunate. One in seven Americans face hunger each day, and we aim to lower that number one box at a time.

How it works

We have partnered with resturaunts in your area. By signing up you will be able to search these buisnesses for meals that are leftover at the end of the day. Once you have selected a meal you want you will pick it up at a preschedualed time at the resturaunt of your choice.

Why Donate?

By redirecting unspoiled food from landfill to our neighbors in need, individuals can support their local communities and reduce environmental impact. Non-perishable and unspoiled perishable food can be donated. Donated food can also include leftovers from events and surplus food inventory. Sign up to donate today!
